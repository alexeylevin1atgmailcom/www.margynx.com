Data Privacy & Security

Agentix Technologies Ltd. complies with Amazon’s Data Protection Policy and Acceptable Use Policy.

Agentix only accesses marketplace data that each seller explicitly authorizes via Amazon OAuth. We do not collect data from any source other than the Amazon Selling Partner API, and we only use the data for the purposes of delivering analytics and features requested by that seller.

Data Segregation: All Amazon seller data is isolated per account. We do not aggregate, merge, cross-reference, or infer insights using data from unrelated sellers or brands. Brand Analytics data from one seller is never used to benefit, compare, or enhance insights for another seller.

No Data Sharing: We do not sell, rent, license, redistribute, disclose, or otherwise share Amazon data with any third parties. If sub-processors are engaged (e.g., hosting providers), they must comply with equal or greater data-protection standards.

Purpose Limitation: Agentix does not use Amazon data to create aggregated market intelligence, competitive benchmarks, or insights that could advantage any seller other than the one who owns the data.

Security: All seller data is encrypted in transit (TLS/HTTPS) and at rest. Access is restricted using least-privilege principles, audited regularly, and protected by role-based controls. Credentials and authentication tokens are stored securely.

Data Retention: Agentix retains seller data only for as long as required to deliver requested services. Upon revocation of authorization or account closure, data is permanently deleted in accordance with Amazon’s Data Protection Policy.
