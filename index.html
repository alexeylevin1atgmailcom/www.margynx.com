Executive story

What this cockpit says in one glance

Growth Health is the central state indicator. Buy Box, Pricing, and Inventory answer whether competition, pricing, or availability are shaping the current outcome. The AI Command Center sits above the panels and turns them into a coherent operating narrative.

Growth Health

State-first panel. Are we healthy, what moved, what broke, and where should leadership focus first.

Buy Box Position

Competitive arena view. It shows whether exposure is slipping because of price pressure, instability, or structural loss.

Pricing Control

Profit-defense layer. It explains whether the organization is disciplined, lagging, or overreacting.

Inventory Risk

Availability layer. It makes stock fragility, days-of-coverage pressure, and revenue-at-risk explicit.

Interaction model

How the layout behaves

The default state shows all four panels at once. Clicking any panel expands it into the dominant view. The other panels collapse into a thin horizontal bus beneath it. The AI Command Center remains fixed.

Default

All four panels visible, equal weight, Command Center on the right.

Large

One panel takes the 2×2 area, others remain live as a thin bus underneath.

Persistent AI rail

The command center does not collapse or move when the selected panel expands.
