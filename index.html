Agentix Seller Cockpit

Agentix Seller Cockpit is an AI-driven analytics, reporting, and performance optimization platform for Amazon Selling Partners. Our solution provides real-time insights using Amazon Selling Partner API roles, including Brand Analytics, Ads, Listings, and Sales reports.

What Agentix Provides

Our platform delivers data-driven insights to help Brands, Private-Label Sellers, and Resellers grow profitably.

  • Brand Analytics Intelligence – market share trends, top search terms, item comparison, repeat purchase rates
  • Buy Box Monitoring & Lost Revenue Analysis
  • Profitability & SKU-level Performance Dashboards
  • Campaign & Advertising Efficiency Metrics
  • Inventory Risk Alerts & Forecasts
  • Historical Reporting and Automated Insights

Data Privacy & Security

Agentix only accesses marketplace data authorized directly by each seller via Amazon OAuth. We do not aggregate, sell, or combine data between unrelated sellers or brands. All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest.
