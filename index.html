Why Margynx

Repricing should understand the business story behind the number.

Most repricers react to competitive signals in isolation. Agentix is built to price with context: whether traffic is softening, Buy Box control is slipping, inventory is getting tight, or margin capture is strong enough to hold the line.

That makes the automation more deliberate. It can push harder when demand is there, defend margin when pressure is weak, and avoid simplistic price cuts when the real issue is availability or conversion.

Product pillars

One cockpit. Five operating surfaces.

Pricing Control

The repricing nerve center. Detect price pressure, find margin room, identify revenue leakage, and drive smarter automation than simple rule-based repricers.

  • Pricing automation and repricing recommendations
  • Competitive position and price pressure detection
  • Margin-aware revenue optimization

Buy Box Position

See where Buy Box share is slipping, how much opportunity is exposed, and where pricing or operational issues are likely driving losses.

  • Buy Box win rate and weighted coverage
  • Lost opportunity visibility
  • Priority areas for corrective action

Growth Health

Connect topline movement to drivers. Understand whether pricing, traffic, conversion, or marketplace dynamics are helping or hurting growth.

  • Executive trend explanations
  • Driver analysis and recommended actions
  • Revenue, sessions, conversion, and margin context

Inventory Risk

Protect the catalog from stock-driven revenue loss while also surfacing excess inventory risk in slower-moving cohorts.

  • Revenue-at-risk alerts
  • Days-of-cover and replenishment signals
  • Availability-aware operating insights

AI Command Center

A coordinating layer that reads the system together and prepares concise operating guidance for humans to review and act on.

  • Cross-module interpretation
  • Action prioritization
  • Human-in-the-loop control

Brand & marketplace analytics

When authorized by the seller, Agentix can incorporate Amazon roles and endpoints such as Brand Analytics, Ads, Listings, and Sales reports.

  • Brand Analytics insights when authorized
  • Listing and sales performance visibility
  • Campaign and marketplace trend context
