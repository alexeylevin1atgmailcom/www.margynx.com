Your Service Name is a data analytics platform designed for Amazon Selling Partners. It helps sellers understand their performance through dashboards, reports, and actionable insights. Our mission is to provide simple, accurate tools for Amazon sellers without sharing or aggregating data across unrelated sellers or brands.

Features

  • Sales performance dashboards
  • SKU-level analytics
  • Inventory alerts
  • Advertising performance summaries
  • Historical reporting

Privacy & Data Use

We access data only with explicit authorization from each Amazon seller through Amazon's secure OAuth workflow. We do not aggregate, share, or combine data between sellers or brands.

All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest. We fully comply with Amazon's Acceptable Use Policy, including sections 4.4 and 4.5 regarding seller data protections.

Security

• TLS/HTTPS for all communication
• Encrypted storage
• Secure authorization through Amazon Selling Partner API (SP-API)
• No third-party sharing of seller data
