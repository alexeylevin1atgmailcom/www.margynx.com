AI Command Center

Work with the cockpit like a strong analyst, marketer, and seller operator.

The Command Center is where the seller speaks to the system in natural language. Ask for diagnosis, request a change, review the rationale, and approve execution when the recommendation makes sense.

Business diagnosis

“Why did margin drop by 5% last week in this product group, and what should I do first to recover it?”

Pricing action

“Reduce the floor price by 1.2% for this SKU list and show me the likely tradeoff before I approve it.”

Guarded automation

“Enable repricing automation for this group to win more Buy Box, but protect margin and avoid widening exposure.”

Recovery path

“Once Buy Box recovers, work prices upward toward a 10% lift while orders hold. If units start to soften, stop and keep the previous level.”

The operator remains in control. The system explains the move, asks for approval, and then carries out the selected action while monitoring outcomes.
