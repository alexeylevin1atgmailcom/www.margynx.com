How it works

1) Seller authorization (OAuth)

Sellers explicitly authorize Agentix to access their data through Amazon’s authorization workflow. Access is scoped to the roles and endpoints required for the service.

2) Analytics & insights

We transform authorized seller data into dashboards, trend analysis, explanations of “why it changed,” and concrete recommendations for action.

3) Alerts & monitoring

Detect anomalies such as buy box loss, conversion drops, inventory risk, and ad efficiency regressions.

4) Retention & deletion controls

Sellers can revoke authorization, and data is deleted according to our retention policy. See Privacy and Security.
