How it works

1) Seller authorization

Sellers explicitly authorize Agentix to access their data through Amazon’s authorization workflow. Access is limited to the roles and endpoints required for the service.

2) Analytics & insights

Authorized seller data is transformed into dashboards, trend explanations, and concrete recommendations.

3) Alerts & monitoring

Detection of anomalies such as Buy Box loss, conversion drops, inventory risk, and ad inefficiencies.

4) Retention & deletion controls

Sellers may revoke authorization at any time. Data handling is described in our Privacy and Security pages.
