Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2025-12-16

Privacy contact: privacy@margynx.com
Support contact: support@margynx.com

1. Who we are

Margynx provides the Agentix Seller Cockpit, an analytics and performance optimization platform for Amazon Selling Partners. This Privacy Policy applies to our website (margynx.com) and to the services provided by Agentix Seller Cockpit.

2. Data we collect and access

Depending on seller authorization and usage of the service, we may process the following categories of data:

  • Account and service data: user email address, organization name, and configuration settings.
  • Seller-authorized Amazon data: operational, performance, and analytical data accessed through Amazon Selling Partner API roles and endpoints explicitly authorized by the seller (such as reports, listings, sales metrics, advertising-related signals where applicable, and Brand Analytics data when authorized).
  • Website technical data: limited technical logs (such as IP address, browser type, and timestamps) collected for security, troubleshooting, and service reliability.

3. How we collect data

  • Seller authorization: Data is accessed only after explicit authorization through Amazon’s authorization workflow.
  • Direct input: Information provided directly by the seller within the service (for example, optional business inputs such as cost data).
  • Automated collection: Limited technical data collected automatically when accessing the website.

4. How we use data

Data is used solely to operate and improve the services requested by the seller, including:

  • Generating dashboards, reports, trend analysis, explanations, and recommendations.
  • Detecting anomalies and providing alerts related to seller performance.
  • Providing customer support and responding to inquiries.
  • Maintaining platform security, reliability, and compliance.

5. Data storage and protection

  • Encryption: Data is protected in transit using HTTPS/TLS and encrypted at rest where stored.
  • Access control: Access is restricted using least-privilege principles and role-based controls.
  • Data segregation: Seller data is logically isolated per seller account.

6. Data sharing and disclosure

Margynx does not sell, rent, or disclose seller-authorized Amazon data to third parties for their independent use. We may use trusted service providers (sub-processors) solely to operate the service, such as hosting or infrastructure providers. These sub-processors are bound by confidentiality and appropriate data protection obligations.

7. Data retention and deletion

Data is retained only for as long as necessary to provide the service and meet legal or security obligations. If a seller revokes authorization or closes their account, seller data is deleted or de-identified within a reasonable timeframe, unless retention is required for legal compliance or security purposes.

8. Seller controls

  • Sellers can revoke authorization through Amazon at any time.
  • Sellers may request account closure or data deletion by contacting us.

9. Legal and regulatory compliance

Margynx is designed to comply with applicable data protection laws and Amazon’s Data Protection Policy, Acceptable Use Policy, and Solution Provider requirements for Selling Partner API applications.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
